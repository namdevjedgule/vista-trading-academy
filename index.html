About Vista Trading Academy

Learn Trading from Real Market Experts

Vista Trading Academy is a professional trading education institute focused on teaching practical strategies for Forex, Crypto, and stock market trading. The academy was founded by experienced trader Rahul Hinge with the mission of making financial market education accessible to everyone.

Our training programs combine market theory with real-world trading techniques so that students can confidently trade in financial markets.

Whether you are a beginner, working professional, student, or business owner, our courses help you build strong trading skills and achieve financial independence.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical knowledge and strategies to succeed in financial markets.

Our Vision

To build a community of skilled traders who use financial markets as a tool for wealth creation.
